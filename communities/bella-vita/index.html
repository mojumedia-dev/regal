About the Community

The Beautiful Life

True to its Italian name, Bella Vita embodies the beautiful life. Set against the backdrop of the Wasatch Mountains in Mapleton, this community offers the perfect blend of natural beauty and refined living.

Each home in Bella Vita is thoughtfully designed with premium finishes, open-concept layouts, and the attention to detail that has defined Regal Homes for over 30 years. Residents enjoy quiet streets, mountain views, and proximity to outdoor recreation, schools, and shopping.
