Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a tour of a Regal Homes community?

You can schedule a tour by calling us at (801) 495-7001, emailing debbie.regalhomes@gmail.com, or filling out the contact form on this page. We will arrange a convenient time to show you available homes and homesites.

What areas does Regal Homes build in?

Regal Homes builds in premium communities across the Wasatch Front and Wasatch Back, including Heber City, Park City, Salt Lake City, Mapleton, Ogden, and West Weber, Utah.

How long does it take to build a Regal Home?

Build times vary based on the home plan and customization level, but most homes are completed within 8-12 months from groundbreaking. Our team keeps you informed with regular progress updates throughout construction.
