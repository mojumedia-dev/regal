Our Building Process

Building your dream home should be exciting, not stressful. Our proven process ensures a seamless experience from your first visit to the day you receive your keys.

01

Discovery and Consultation

Your journey begins with a personal consultation. We take the time to understand your lifestyle, needs, and vision for your new home. Together, we will explore our active communities, review available homesites, and discuss floorplan options that align with your goals.

This is where the relationship begins -- and at Regal Homes, relationships are at the heart of everything we do.

02

Design Center Experience

This is where your home becomes uniquely yours. In our Design Center, you will work alongside professional designers who guide you through every selection -- from cabinetry and countertops to flooring, fixtures, and finishes.

Our curated selection process ensures you can personalize your home to reflect your taste and lifestyle, with expert guidance to help you make confident choices.

03

Construction

With your selections finalized, construction begins. Our experienced team manages every detail, from foundation to finishing touches, with the quality craftsmanship Regal Homes has been known for since 1994.

Throughout the build, you will receive regular progress updates and have opportunities to walk your home at key milestones. Transparency is one of our core values -- you will always know where your home stands.

04

Welcome Home

The day you have been waiting for. After a thorough walkthrough and quality inspection, you receive the keys to your new Regal Home. But our relationship does not end at closing -- we stand behind our homes with comprehensive warranty coverage and responsive service.

Welcome to the Regal Homes family.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the home building process take with Regal Homes?

Most Regal Homes are completed within 8-12 months from groundbreaking, depending on the floorplan and level of customization. Our team provides regular updates throughout construction.

What does semi-custom mean?

Semi-custom means you start with a proven floorplan and then personalize it through our Design Center. You choose finishes, colors, fixtures, and upgrades to make the home uniquely yours, without the complexity and cost of a fully custom build.

Can I make changes during construction?

We accommodate changes when possible, especially early in the process. Your project manager will advise on timing and any cost implications of modifications.

Do I need to have my own land to build with Regal Homes?

No. Regal Homes builds exclusively in our own planned communities. We offer premium lots in five active communities across the Wasatch Front and Back.
